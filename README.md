## 🧠 What We Do

Most companies sit on thousands of documents, reports, and web pages — but finding a specific answer still means searching manually. **We fix that.**

We specialize in **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)** — the technology that lets AI read your documents and answer questions from them with precision and source citations.

> 💡 Think of it as a genius assistant who has read every file in your organization and can answer any question about them instantly — in plain language, with sources.
