### 4️⃣ Natural Language Processing (NLP)

> *Turn unstructured text into structured business intelligence.*

We apply state-of-the-art NLP to extract meaning, structure, and value from text at scale — across languages and domains.

- **Sentiment Analysis** — Understand how customers feel across reviews, emails, and support tickets
- **Named Entity Recognition** — Extract names, dates, amounts, and locations from documents automatically
- **Text Classification** — Auto-categorize thousands of documents, emails, or tickets instantly
- **Summarization Pipelines** — Condense long reports into executive briefs automatically
- **Custom Fine-Tuning** — Adapt LLMs to your specific domain vocabulary and tone
- **Multilingual NLP** — Arabic, German, French, English and more

---

### 5️⃣ Computer Vision & Document Intelligence

> *Give your systems the ability to see and understand.*

We build vision-based AI for businesses that deal with images, video streams, or visual documents.

- **Document OCR & Extraction** — Convert scanned PDFs, forms, and invoices into clean structured data
- **Visual Inspection Systems** — Detect defects or quality issues on manufacturing lines in real time
- **Image Classification & Tagging** — Auto-organize and label large image datasets
- **Object Detection** — Identify and locate objects in real-time video feeds
- **Form & Table Extraction** — Pull structured data out of unstructured visual documents
- **ID & Document Verification** — Automated identity document processing pipelines

---

### 6️⃣ Data Engineering & MLOps

> *Garbage in, garbage out. We make sure your AI eats clean data.*

AI is only as good as what it's fed. We build the infrastructure that makes AI reliable in production — not just in demos.

- **ETL Pipelines** — Clean, transform, and route data to where AI needs it
- **Vector Database Architecture** — Pinecone, FAISS, pgvector setup and optimization
- **Model Deployment** — Docker, FastAPI, cloud — your model live and stable at scale
- **Monitoring & Alerting** — Know the moment your AI drifts, fails, or degrades
- **Data Quality Pipelines** — Automated validation so bad data never reaches your AI
- **Embedding Pipelines at Scale** — Index millions of documents efficiently
